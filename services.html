FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I have my colour done on my first visit?

    YES, absolutely! This is possible if a colour skin test has been completed at least 48 hours prior to your appointment.

  • What is the youngest age to colour hair?

    Colour services are available for age 16 years and over.

  • Can I have just my hair coloured at an appointment?

    All colour services must be accompanied with a finishing Blow Dry or a Cut and Blow Dry.

  • I am starting Chemotherapy and worried about losing my hair?

    As a MyNewHair Ambassador, I am here to provide advice, help & support to anyone living with the effects of medical hair loss. I understand hair loss can affect people on many different levels, both emotionally and physically. I hope, through the help that I can provide, I will aid and inspire you to have the hair you want and deserve.

Colour Inspiration

Whether you’re looking for a bold change or a natural refresh, I’ll guide you to colour that fits your routine and hair health. As a L'Oreal Colour Specialist, I keep techniques current so your colour looks refined and lasts.

Things I consider

  • Tonal drift: soft root stretch into ombre that grows out beautifully.
  • Multi-tonal effect: layered shades to add depth and texture.
  • Freehand application: painted placement for custom dimension.
  • Service time: how long you’ll be in the chair from application to shampoo.
  • Hair contouring: face-framing brightness tailored to your features.
  • Base & regrowth: natural shade, previous colour, and how it grows through.
  • Tint vs gloss: permanent coverage or semi-permanent shine and tone.
  • Lifting/darkening: adjusting a couple of shades lighter or deeper with care.

Skin Test

A quick consultation keeps your colour experience safe and comfortable. I follow L'Oreal Professionnel’s protocol to decide when a skin allergy test is required.

What happens

  • Professional consult: we review your colour history and sensitivities.
  • Skin test: performed when needed to check for allergic response.
  • Application plan: tailored approach for your service on the day.

A skin allergy test must be completed 48 hours before your appointment.

Ladies Cuts

From cropped shapes to long, voluminous layers, I tailor each cut to your face shape, texture, and lifestyle.

How we work

  • Shape first: bobs, layers, or structured crops designed around you.
  • Texture-aware: adjustments that boost movement, volume, and ease.
  • Maintenance: visits every 4–6 weeks to keep the cut looking refined.
