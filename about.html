About

Meet Adam Vincent

L’Oréal Colour Specialist, educator, and trusted stylist for clients who want beautiful, wearable hair.

With over two decades in professional hairdressing, Adam Vincent has built a reputation for exceptional colour work, precision cutting, and heartfelt client care. His passion lies in helping people look and feel amazing, whether through creative transformations or subtle, confidence-boosting changes.

Adam’s career began in Bristol, where he grew a thriving franchise salon and trained more than 100 stylists. As a qualified TAQA educator and L’Oréal Colour Specialist, he has led award-winning teams to recognition such as the South West L’Oréal Colour Trophy and Bristol’s Best Hair Salon.

A personal approach

“Since becoming a father in 2015, flexibility and balance have become central to how I work. My daughter, Florence, inspires me daily to stay creative and grounded. I believe everyone deserves a hair experience that fits their life, whether that’s in the salon, at home, or online.”

At Adam Vincent Hair, you’ll always receive professional service with genuine attention to detail, care, and flexibility.
